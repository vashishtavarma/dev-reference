Microsoft Outlook - Email & Calendar Management

## What is Microsoft Outlook?
Microsoft Outlook is an email client and personal information manager that includes email, calendar, task management, and contact organization features.

## Key Features
- Email management with folders and rules
- Calendar scheduling and meeting invitations
- Contact management and address books
- Task and to-do list organization
- Integration with Microsoft Office suite
- Mobile and web access

## Why Use Microsoft Outlook?
- Industry standard for corporate email
- Seamless integration with Exchange servers
- Advanced email organization and filtering
- Unified calendar and scheduling system
- Enterprise security and compliance features

## Common Use Cases
- Corporate email communication
- Meeting scheduling and calendar management
- Task tracking and reminders
- Contact database management
- Email automation and rules setup
