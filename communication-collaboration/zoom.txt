Zoom - Video Conferencing & Webinar Platform

## What is Zoom?
Zoom is a cloud-based video conferencing platform that enables virtual meetings, webinars, and collaboration across devices and locations.

## Key Features
- HD video and audio conferencing
- Screen sharing and annotation
- Recording and transcription
- Breakout rooms for group discussions
- Webinar hosting capabilities
- Mobile and desktop applications

## Why Use Zoom?
- Reliable video quality and connection
- Easy-to-use interface for all users
- Supports large-scale meetings and webinars
- Cross-platform compatibility
- Strong security features and encryption

## Common Use Cases
- Client meetings and presentations
- All-hands company meetings
- Training sessions and webinars
- Remote interviews and onboarding
- Virtual events and conferences
