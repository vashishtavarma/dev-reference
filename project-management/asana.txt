Asana - Task & Project Management Platform

## What is Asana?
Asana is a web-based project management tool that helps teams organize, track, and manage their work and projects through tasks, projects, and team collaboration features.

## Key Features
- Task creation and assignment
- Project timelines and Gantt charts
- Team collaboration and comments
- Custom fields and project templates
- Progress tracking and reporting
- Calendar and timeline views

## Why Use Asana?
- Intuitive interface for project organization
- Flexible project views (list, board, timeline)
- Strong team collaboration features
- Integrates with popular business tools
- Scales from small teams to enterprises

## Common Use Cases
- Sprint planning and task management
- Marketing campaign coordination
- Product roadmap planning
- Team goal tracking and OKRs
- Cross-functional project collaboration
